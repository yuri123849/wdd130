Raft & Roar

At Raft & Roar, our purpose is to connect people with the raw power and beauty of nature through exhilarating whitewater experiences. Our mission is to provide safe, unforgettable adventures while preserving the rivers we love through sustainable practices. We live by the creed: "Respect the river, challenge yourself, and leave no trace." Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned thrill-seeker, our motto says it all: "Ride the wild. Roar with life."

History

Founded in 1995 by river enthusiasts Jake Morrow and Lila Chen, Raft & Roar began as a small whitewater rafting operation in Colorado, fueled by a passion for adventure and a love of the wild. What started with just two rafts and a handful of daring clients quickly grew into a respected name in the industry, known for thrilling rapids, expert guides, and unforgettable experiences. Over the years, Raft & Roar expanded to new rivers, introduced eco-conscious practices, and built a loyal community of adventurers. Today, the company continues to push boundaries while staying true to its roots—delivering heart-pounding excitement on the water.
